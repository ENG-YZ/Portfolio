01
AI Telegram Audio Summarizer
n8n Telegram Bot API OpenAI Whisper GPT-4 Notion API
The Problem
A team manager was spending 45+ minutes daily listening to voice messages from clients and team members, then manually typing summaries into Notion — a slow, tedious bottleneck.
The Solution
A Telegram bot receives audio messages, transcribes them via Whisper AI, generates structured summaries with GPT-4, and saves them automatically to Notion — all within 30 seconds of receiving the message.
Result → Daily audio processing reduced from 45 minutes to under 2 minutes. Manager now reviews clean AI summaries instead of replaying voice messages.
02
AI Document Summarizer
n8n GPT-4 Google Drive Telegram Bot Airtable
The Problem
A legal team received 20–30 PDF contracts daily and needed key clauses, risks, and deadlines extracted from each one before attorney review — a process that took hours manually.
The Solution
An automated pipeline monitors a Google Drive folder, extracts PDF text, sends it to GPT-4 with a custom legal prompt, outputs structured summaries to Airtable, and notifies the team via Telegram instantly.
Result → Pre-review time cut by 70%. Attorneys receive structured 1-page AI briefs instead of reading full contracts cold.
03
Gmail Automation System
n8n Gmail API GPT-4 Google Sheets Slack
The Problem
An e-commerce founder was manually reading, sorting, and replying to 80–100 emails per day across customer support, suppliers, and partners — consuming 3–4 hours every single day.
The Solution
Full Gmail automation: AI reads and labels incoming email, drafts context-aware replies, routes urgent items to Slack, logs structured data to Google Sheets, and delivers a daily digest summary each morning.
Result → Email time dropped from 3.5 hours to 30 minutes per day. 90% of routine emails handled autonomously — founder now reviews, not writes.
